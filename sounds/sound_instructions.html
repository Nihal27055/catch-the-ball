Sound Files for Catch the Ball Game

Download these three sound files and save them in your "sounds" folder to add audio to your game!

1. catch.mp3

This sound plays when you successfully catch a balloon.

Recommended: A short "pop" or "ding" sound

Download catch.mp3

2. miss.mp3

This sound plays when a balloon touches the red line and you lose a life.

Recommended: A short negative sound effect

Download miss.mp3

3. levelup.mp3

This sound plays when you reach a new level in the game.

Recommended: A celebratory chime or fanfare sound

Download levelup.mp3
