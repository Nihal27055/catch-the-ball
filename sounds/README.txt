Sound Credits:

You need to add your own sound files in this folder:

1. catch.mp3 - Sound played when the player catches a balloon
2. miss.mp3 - Sound played when a balloon touches the red line
3. levelup.mp3 - Sound played when the player levels up

You can find free sound effects at:
- https://freesound.org/
- https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/
- https://www.zapsplat.com/

Recommended sounds:
- For catch.mp3: A short "pop" or "ding" sound
- For miss.mp3: A short "negative" sound effect
- For levelup.mp3: A celebratory chime or fanfare sound

Place your MP3 files directly in this folder and ensure they match the filenames exactly.
